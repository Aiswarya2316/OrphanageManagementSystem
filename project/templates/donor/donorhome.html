Care For Child

"Orphanage management" refers to the comprehensive oversight and operation of a residential facility that provides care, shelter, and support to orphaned or otherwise vulnerable children. It encompasses aspects like daily living needs, education, healthcare, emotional well-being, and ultimately aims to prepare them for a stable future through fostering a nurturing environment and facilitating adoption or independent living opportunities when appropriate."
